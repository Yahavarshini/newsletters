The Problem We're Solving
Telephony calling was broken for WhatsApp businesses
Traditional telephony support creates a fragmented, confusing experience — for both agents and customers.
Before
  • Each agent needed a separate phone number — customers received calls from unknown numbers and suspected spam
  • The WhatsApp bot number and the support phone number were different — customers got confused
  • Customers had to call the business — long wait times made them frustrated
  • Agents had zero chat context on the call — had to ask the customer to repeat everything
  • Nothing was captured after the call — no record, no transcript, no accountability
Now with WhatsApp Calling
  • Calls come from the same verified WhatsApp Business number — customers recognise and trust who is calling
  • One number for everything — chat, bot, and call — no confusion, no spam flags
  • Business calls the customer proactively, with their permission — faster resolution
  • Agent has full chat history visible during the call — no need to repeat anything
  • Every call is recorded and transcribed automatically — full record attached to the thread
Why we ask for permission before calling
Unlike a cold call, WhatsApp Business Calling starts with a consent request sent to the customer over WhatsApp. The customer taps Allow or Don't Allow — and only if they approve does the agent get to call them.
How It Works
From chat to call — in one seamless flow
1
Customer raises a query on WhatsApp
The conversation happens in the usual chat thread. If the query is complex, the agent decides a call would resolve it faster.
2
Agent sends a Call Permission Request
With one click, the agent sends a WhatsApp message asking: "Can we call you to help resolve this faster?" The customer taps Allow or Don't Allow.
3
Agent initiates the call from the dashboard
Once approved, the agent clicks Call inside Engage AI. The call rings on the customer's WhatsApp from the same verified business number.
4
Call is logged — initiated, in-progress, ended
The conversation thread captures: Call Initiated → Call Accepted → Call Ended — all visible in the same chat.
5
Call Summary — recording + transcript attached
A call summary is automatically generated — recording, full transcript with speaker attribution, and call duration — linked to the conversation thread.
